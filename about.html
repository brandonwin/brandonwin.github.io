Hey, I'm Brandon Win.

I'm a junior majoring in Integrated Digital Media at NYU, and I'm primarily interested in UI/UX and game design opportunities. I thrive when I design things I love and personally attach to, and I strive to make memorable products.

In 2018, I traveled from my hometown of Walnut, California to New York City in pursuit of my love for game design. I learned to love UI/UX at the same time I learned to love halal carts.

In my free time, I enjoy watching YouTube video essays, analyzing music lyrics, playing games with friends, and finding the next song to add to my playlist.
