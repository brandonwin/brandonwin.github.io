Hi, I'm Brandon!

I'm a product designer with 3 years of experience. I chose to pursue product design (and design in a broader sense) because I love building and admiring intentionally crafted design, despite whether it's readily apparent to the user or not.

Since graduating from New York University in 2022, I've directed and shaped end-to-end user experiences at startups, sometimes even while being their first design hire. Additionally, I've started my own design studio, BNWIN Design, to branch out career-wise and design for those in my local community.

I love studying design in UX, video games, and the real world. In my spare time, I love watching video essays and eating shumai.
