Building Design at Sideline

Year
2025
Disciplines
Product design
Design systems
Web design

At Sideline, I was in charge of building the website and visual style, and I worked with the founder to create a positive, welcoming message inviting young, underserved entrepreneurs to sign up for free mentorship.

Background

Sideline is a pre-seed company that provides mentorship services for first-time entrepreneurs in underserved, local communities.

The company needed a product designer who was able to develop product features and build a visual style that would be reflected across the product.

My Role

I serve as the founding product designer who built the website, product workflows, and visual style.

The version being showcased here was from September 2025.
