FurnishStore

Problem: Design a way to purchase furniture and have it delivered straight to your front door.

Solution: I designed FurnishStore, a mobile application to empower and support local furniture businesses while providing an alternative to purchasing furniture for users. For many college students, buying furniture is either IKEA or nothing. FurnishStore aims to position itself as an alternative for college students and young professionals looking to furnish their living space, whether it's their first apartment or fourth. I fully designed high-fidelity wireframes prototyping the shopping, checkout and delivery process for customers.

FurnishStore is a UX/UI design project that empowers local furniture businesses, such as DIY creators, family businesses and local shops, to be able to put their product on an attractive, modern platform. Customers are able to support local businesses, have furniture delivered to their front door, and create their own unique styles to share with fellow users.

I initially created the idea for FurnishStore out of frustration towards the lack of viable options regarding furniture: for many college students, it's either IKEA or bust. Realizing the opportunity to disrupt the market and provide an alternative for consumers, I conducted user research on college students and their core needs on their furniture-purchasing process. I was able to produce a user journey and conduct research that I took into account during my design process.

I designed FurnishStore with the aim to ease the furniture shopping process for college students and older, utilizing a sleek and minimalist design that puts the user at ease but simulatenously doesn't lack the necessary information regarding the process itself. I created a collection function, which allows users to build moodboards/shopping carts filled with available products.

This was a comprehensive UX/UI design project that I managed through Asana and created over the course of 6 months. All Figma wireframes are available via the button above.
