heebiekeebies

Brand & web design for a mechanical keyboard Shopify site.

Click this to go to heebiekeebies.

During summer 2020, I performed contracted work for my friend and his online business heebiekeebies. I was presented with extensive tasks of brand design, web design and product photography for all of his products. Along with that, I had to also learn the Shopify interface and work with its extensive code. However, I was more than excited for my first professional work in my field.

I kept near-constant communication with the client due to how much work this project would require. I organized several meetups with my client to discuss the project and also perform product photography shoots for all of his products.

After product photography, I started designing the website while communicating with the client about my process, since due to the project being highly time-sensitive, communication was a must. I designed the website around the product, since I found that I wanted to create an interface that would be most conducive to clients wanting his product, adding a testimonial section, clear button interactivity, and large image showcases.
