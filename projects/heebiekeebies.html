heebiekeebies

Getting your keyboard parts with ease.

heebiekeebies is an e-commerce website selling quality mechanical keyboard parts with fast shipping. I was contracted to design and develop their Shopify site launch, creating product materials, designing the overall look and feel of the entire site, and developing an internal system that the client can expand the website with.

Product

Website, E-Commerce

Technologies

Key Services

Web Design, UI Design, Brand Design, Ad Design, Product Photography

The Problem

heebiekeebies needs an online shopping site to conduct business on. They currently operate on a Discord server, and they need a large scale website to accompany it.

The Solution

Build a Shopify site to accomodate all parts of the shopping process (product selection, shopping cart, checkout, etc.) for heebiekeebies customers.

The User

Clarence, 26, Software Engineer

Clarence is looking for new additions to his mechanical keyboard collection. He's a burgeoning enthusiast of the hobby, but he's not looking to break the bank. Clarence wants to purchase parts and make sure it's a trustworthy and seamless process.

"I'm really into mechanical keyboards, but so many sites are expensive and take way too long to ship my purchases. This hobby is getting popular, but why doesn't the convenience factor catch up?"

Motivations

  • Expand his keyboard collection with quality products
  • Know how fast his products will get to him
  • Save money

Discoveries

Early Findings

  • Catch customer attention quickly. Many mechanical keyboard enthusiasts purchase from different providers based on word of mouth. Creating an interface that will catch their eye to support a smaller vendor is important.
  • Site functionality and usability are key. If the site is difficult to navigate or buggy with its code, customers are more than likely to abandon ship and switch to another vendor.
  • Quality graphics and visuals are high priority. If the products look good, customers will want to make their workspaces look good with them. High quality product photography shoots will be important to conduct.

Product Pivots

  • Create a detailed project list of all tasks in order of importance leading up to site launch.
  • Distinguish the heebiekeebies brand from competitors through copywriting and visual identity.
  • Communicate with the client to start website design, development and implementation.

Iteration

Information Architecture

I was given a large set of products that would be available on launch, and I designed information architecture (product tags, catalogues, etc.) to properly organize all of these products on the site. I created back-end structures that the client would continue using after my contract, and I also designed call-to-actions (CTAs) that would navigate the user to key products via the front page.

Style Guide

I collaborated with heebiekeebies's visual designer to create the website's style guide and branding. I advocated for the user during the design process, making sure that the website's style guide would meet accessibility standards.

Learnings

  1. Transparent and constant feedback with the client is core. As this was my first large design contract, I learned how to conduct feedback review within a client-designer relationship. Constantly communicating with my client on whether they liked my designs (whether they were work-in-progress or being finalized) were key in the design process.
  1. There's always something new to learn in web design. This contract was my first professional foray into Shopify development, and learning the API was difficult to say the least. I constantly was searching on how to create specific interactions or features (such as shipping insurance) that my client required, and I was able to learn a lot during the contract.
  1. Interactions with designers and engineers are great for learning new designs. My client was from a SDE background, and my client had a creative lead working on the website launch. Working as the web designer between the creative lead and my client was a fulfilling experience to touch upon nearly all aspects of the product launch.
